CYCLONE

a three.js tribute to Vortex Software’s 1985 ZX Spectrum classic

From the decrypted ROM – “COLLECT FIVE CRATES AND RETURN TO BASE”. You’re the pilot of a rescue helicopter over the Cyclone archipelago (BASE, BANANA, KOKOLA, LAGOON, PEAK, GILLIGANS, RED, SKEG, BONE, CLAW, ENTERPRISE…).

A massive cyclone is sweeping through — land beside a crate to pick it up, then return to the red & white helipad on BASE to deliver it. Wind speed increases as you near the cyclone: the closer you fly, the more it buffets you away.

W forward thrust · A/D turn left / right · Space/Shift climb / descend. Controls are mapped to the ROM's FORWARD / TURN / UP / DOWN buttons — there’s no strafing in the original.
